Problems

I took about 2 whole days to try and recreate this webpage, so I did ran into quite a few problems trying to do so. Something I had a difficult time trying ot recreate were the images inside of the numbers, with the gradient effect and having it not move from its position, when resizing the page. To figure this problem out I inspected my browser, seeing what I could do to apply this effect and not have the images change when resizing the page. I spent time going back and forward, seeing what worked and what didn't. I was finally able to figure out how to apply the effect, by having each individual image have a specific background size and a background clip. This gave me the effect of the images being inside of the numbers and when resizing it, they wont move at all. Another problem I ran into was figuring out how have the little triangle in the menu appear centered. I tried everything and it didn't seem to budge from its area. Unfortunately I was not able to figure out how to make the triangle appear centered, but hopefully, with more practice, I could get it looking exactly like the original. *(^o^)*
