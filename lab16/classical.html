♪ Classical Music ♫

🎹 Meaning 📖

It is known for its sophisticated instrumental music. It can contain a mixture of instrumentals and vocal style.
For a more specific definition...
It is a type of music written in the European tradition during periods 1750-1830.

🤔 Whate are the benefits ❔

Both people who listen and play classical music tend to lower their stress levels. Whenever working on homework and wanting to get assignments done, people tend to put music in the background to get them motivated, that can range in styles from pop, classical, jazz, and etc.

🎼 Types of classical music 🎶

When hearing the word Classical Music, you might think there is only one form for it.
In the matter of fact, that is wrong. ❌
There are multiple forms of classical music.
Some of them are:

  • Sonata
  • Concerto
  • Opera
  • Chamber Music
  • Symphony
  • Overtue
